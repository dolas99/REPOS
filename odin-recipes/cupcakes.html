Delicious Chocolate Cupcakes

About these Cupcakes

These chocolate cupcakes are soft, moist, and full of chocolatey goodness. Perfect for parties, snacks, or dessert cravings!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F) and line a cupcake tin with paper liners.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, cream the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually mix in the dry ingredients, alternating with milk, until smooth batter forms.
  6. Divide the batter evenly among the cupcake liners (about 2/3 full).
  7. Bake for 18–22 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  8. Let cool completely, then frost as desired.
